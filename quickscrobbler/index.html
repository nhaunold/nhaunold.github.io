What's this?

QuickScrobbler is an application for scrobbling music to the popular internet community Last.fm. It's tiny, runs in the background and uses barely any resources. It currently supports iTunes and a small subset of features such as the ability to love songs, love tracks rated ★★★★★ automatically and incognito mode to hide your day-to-day guilty pleasures from your friends. Oh, and the best part? It's free.
